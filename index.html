NIL questions, answered

What is NIL?

NIL stands for Name, Image & Likeness. Since 2021, NCAA athletes (and high-schoolers in many states) can earn money from endorsements, social posts, autographs, appearances and more — without losing eligibility.

How accurate is this calculator?

It produces a data-informed estimate based on the factors brands actually weigh, calibrated to publicly reported deal ranges. Real offers vary with timing, relationships and negotiation. Treat it as a starting benchmark, not a quote.

Why does engagement matter more than followers?

Brands pay for attention that converts. A 50k-follower account with 6% engagement can be worth more than a 200k account with 0.5% engagement, because more of the audience actually sees and acts on content.

Do high-school athletes have NIL value?

Yes — many states now permit high-school NIL deals. Local and regional brands, camps and gear companies are common partners.

Is my data stored?

Your calculator inputs stay in your browser. We only collect an email if you choose to request your full report. See our Privacy Policy.
